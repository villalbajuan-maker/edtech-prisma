# Brand Narrative

## PRISMA

PRISMA reveals institutional realities through multiple dimensions.

It turns responses into diagnostic clarity, profiles, priorities, and recommended action.

## Core Message

Institutions do not need more forms.

They need instruments that help them decide.

## Positioning

PRISMA is a library of digital diagnostic instruments for institutional decisions.
