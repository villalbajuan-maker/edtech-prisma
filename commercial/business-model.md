# Business Model

## Core Economic Asset

The core asset is the diagnostic instrument.

PRISMA designs, prepares, structures, implements, and commercializes digital diagnostic instruments.

## Possible Revenue Models

## 1. Instrument License

An institution licenses one instrument for a period, cohort, or application volume.

## 2. Pay Per Application Or Cohort

An institution pays to apply an instrument to a specific cohort.

## 3. Collection Subscription

An institution subscribes to a collection such as PRISMA Higher Education.

## 4. Institutional Subscription

An institution pays for access based on volume, roles, reporting, and number of instruments.

## 5. Add-ons

Optional paid add-ons:

- setup support;
- institutional customization;
- executive interpretation session;
- Companion;
- benchmarking;
- follow-up applications;
- integration;
- white label.

## Commercial Principle

Do not sell questions.

Sell decision-ready diagnostic instruments.
