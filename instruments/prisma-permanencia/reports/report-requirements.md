# Report Requirements

PRISMA Permanencia reports must translate diagnostic results into decisions.

## Student-Level Report

Optional depending on institutional configuration.

Should include:

- preparation summary;
- dimension levels;
- recommended next steps;
- support-seeking guidance;
- non-stigmatizing language.

## Institutional Executive Report

For rectors, vice-rectors, deans, directors, and planning teams.

Should include:

- cohort overview;
- priority distribution;
- strongest and weakest dimensions;
- risk profile distribution;
- comparisons by program, campus, modality, or jornada;
- strategic recommendations;
- resource allocation implications.

## Operational Report

For permanence, welfare, academic, and financial support teams.

Should include:

- prioritized student list;
- dominant profile;
- vulnerable dimensions;
- recommended route;
- suggested responsible area;
- action type;
- follow-up flag.

## Reporting Principle

No report should merely describe results.

Every report should help the institution decide what to do next.
