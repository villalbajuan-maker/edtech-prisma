# Diagnostic Dimensions

PRISMA Permanencia V1 should diagnose the student's entry conditions across multiple dimensions.

## Proposed Dimensions

1. Vocational clarity
2. Expectations about university life
3. Study habits and self-regulation
4. Perceived academic preparation
5. Time availability and external load
6. Financial pressure
7. Support network
8. Non-clinical emotional adaptation
9. Initial sense of belonging
10. Permanence intention and early doubts

## Design Principle

The instrument should not reduce permanence risk to academic performance.

It must capture conditions that usually appear before grades, absence, financial arrears, or formal withdrawal.
