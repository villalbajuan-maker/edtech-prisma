6. PRISMA Empleabilidad

## Instrument 1: PRISMA Permanencia

Purpose:

Diagnose preparation, early fragility, permanence risk, and accompaniment needs in incoming students.

Primary buyer:

- rector;
- academic vice-rector;
- student success or permanence director;
- welfare director;
- planning or quality director;
- dean or program director in high-risk programs.

Primary users:

- permanence teams;
- welfare teams;
- academic coordinators;
- faculties;
- financial support offices.

Core output:

- preparation dimensions;
- priority of accompaniment;
- dominant risk profile;
- recommended support route;
- aggregate cohort/program report.
