# Decision Log

This file records strategic decisions so the project does not drift.

## 2026-04-18

### Decision: PRISMA is a separate business/product line

PRISMA will not be mixed with the existing EduTech Marketing repository.

### Decision: The business model centers on instruments

The core economic asset is the diagnostic instrument, not consulting hours.

Services may exist as add-ons, but the primary business is the design, preparation, implementation structure, and commercialization of high-quality digital diagnostic instruments.

### Decision: First market focus is higher education

The first target segment is Colombian universities and higher education institutions.

### Decision: First instrument is PRISMA Permanencia

The first instrument focuses on preparation, early risk, student permanence, and routes of accompaniment for incoming students.
