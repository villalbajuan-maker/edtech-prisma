# Category Thesis

## Category Name

Digital diagnostic instruments for high-cost institutional decisions.

Spanish working language:

Instrumentos diagnosticos digitales para decisiones institucionales de alto costo.

## Strategic Claim

Many institutions already collect data, run surveys, and generate reports. The failure is not data collection. The failure is that institutional information often does not become timely, differentiated, and operational decision-making.

PRISMA exists to build diagnostic instruments that encode methodology, scoring, interpretation, and recommendations into reusable digital assets.

## What PRISMA Is Not

PRISMA is not:

- a generic survey platform;
- a quiz builder;
- a traditional consulting firm;
- a psychological testing company;
- an AI chatbot product;
- a dashboard-first analytics tool.

## What PRISMA Is

PRISMA is a library of high-quality institutional diagnostic instruments.

Each instrument is designed around:

- a clear institutional problem;
- a defined target population;
- diagnostic dimensions;
- structured questions;
- scoring logic;
- profiles or levels;
- actionable interpretation;
- recommendations;
- reporting;
- responsible use boundaries.

## Initial Market Focus

The first market is higher education institutions in Colombia.

The first decision field is student permanence, preparation, and early risk detection.

The first instrument is PRISMA Permanencia.

## Strategic Bet

If PRISMA can build one instrument that is credible, useful, repeatable, and commercially attractive for universities and higher education institutions, it can become the foundation for a broader instrument library across education, corporate, social impact, and institutional markets.
