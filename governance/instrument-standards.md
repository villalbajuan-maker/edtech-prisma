# Instrument Standards

Every PRISMA instrument must meet these standards before commercialization.

## 1. Purpose Clarity

The instrument must solve a specific institutional decision problem.

## 2. Diagnostic Architecture

The instrument must define:

- dimensions;
- subdimensions;
- item types;
- scoring;
- interpretation;
- profiles or levels;
- recommendations.

## 3. Actionability

The instrument must produce outputs that help an institution decide what to do.

## 4. Responsible Language

The instrument must avoid deterministic, stigmatizing, or clinically inappropriate labels.

## 5. Traceability

The logic from question to score to recommendation must be explainable.

## 6. Versioning

Each instrument must have versions and change notes.

## 7. Privacy

Student and institutional data must be handled with explicit access boundaries and responsible use.
